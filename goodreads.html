Roland's bookshelf: read

East of Eden
by John Steinbeck
Beautiful. Steinbeck was right to call it “the first book.” Joyous and triumphant while at the same time deeply melancholic and raw. A perfect ending. Biblical, but better than the Bible. Easily in my top 3.
Ubik
by Philip K. Dick
Best PKD I’ve read so far. Very indecisive plot. Would like to see film adaptation
